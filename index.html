Yunho Oh

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at EPFL (PI: Professor Babak Falsafi). I completed Ph.D. in the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Yonsei University (Advisor: Professor Won Woo Ro). My research interests include datacenter architecture, high-performance GPU architecture, in-storage processing architecture, energy-efficient heterogeneous multi-core architecture, memory system, and high-performance computing.
From August 2016 to August 2017, I worked as a visiting graduate scholar at the University of Southern California (Faculty Sponsor: Professor Murali Annavaram). From 2011 to 2014, I worked as a software engineer at Mobile Communications Buniness, Samsung Electronics.
